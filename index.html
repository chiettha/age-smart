Ageing Groups' Experience with Sleep Monitoring And Related Technologies

Woolcock Institute of Medical Research

About the Study

Sleep plays an important role in keeping our brain healthy as we get older.

This study looks at how older adults feel about using digital technologies, such as apps and wearable devices, that can track sleep and help support health management.

Who Can Participate?

Aged 50 years or older

Live in Australia

Experience sleep disturbance

Learn More

What does participation involve?

You will complete a 15-minute online anonymous survey with brief battery of questionnaires.

(Optional) You may also be asked to trial sleep technology devices at home and attend two 60-90 minute focus group sessions at the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research.

Participants who complete and submit a survey response will receive a chance to win one of four $100 gift cards if interested. Focus group participants will additionally receive a $50 gift card upon study completion.

A short screening questionnaire (3 minutes) will determine eligibility.
